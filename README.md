# 📊 Sales Trend & Performance Analysis Dashboard

## 📌 Overview
This project is an interactive **Microsoft Excel Dashboard** designed to analyze sales performance, customer behavior, product demand, delivery efficiency, and discount impact. It transforms raw sales data into actionable business insights through dynamic KPIs, charts, and slicers.

## 🎯 Objectives
- Monitor overall sales performance
- Analyze sales trends and discount impact
- Identify top customers and products
- Evaluate delivery performance
- Compare sales across cities and demographics

## 📂 Dataset
- **500 Sales Records**
- **30 Attributes**
- Covers customer, product, order, delivery, and financial information
- Includes derived fields such as Year, Month, and Delivery Status

## 📊 Key KPIs
- 💰 Total Sales
- 🚚 Average Delivery Time
- 🛒 Most Ordered Product
- 👥 Average Customer Age
- 🎁 Total Discount Offered

## 📈 Dashboard Features
- Sales vs Discount Trend Analysis
- City-wise Sales Performance
- Top Customers Analysis
- Gender-wise Monthly Sales
- Top Products Overview
- Delivery Performance Tracking

## 🎛 Interactive Filters
- Year
- Month
- City
- Gender
- Delivery Status

## 🛠 Tools & Skills Used
- Microsoft Excel
- Pivot Tables & Pivot Charts
- Slicers
- KPI Reporting
- Data Visualization
- Business Analytics

## 🎓 Outcome
Developed a dynamic dashboard that enables stakeholders to track performance, uncover trends, and make data-driven business decisions efficiently.
